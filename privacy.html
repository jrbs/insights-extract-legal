Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Insights Extract ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application and its integration with third-party platforms, including TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Insights Extract, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your display name and basic profile information provided via TikTok OAuth (scope: user.info.basic).
  • Usage data: Interaction logs such as features accessed and timestamps, used solely to improve the service.
  • Content you provide: Videos or media you choose to upload or publish through the App.

2. TikTok Content Post API — Data Handling

Insights Extract integrates with the TikTok Content Post API to allow users to publish content directly to their TikTok accounts. We handle this data with strict care:

  • No permanent storage of videos: We do not store permanent copies of any video content processed through the TikTok Content Post API. Video data is held in memory only for the duration required to complete the upload and is immediately discarded afterward.
  • Encrypted access tokens: All OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens issued by TikTok are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption and transmitted exclusively over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). Tokens are never logged, exposed in URLs, or stored in plain text.
  • Minimal data retention: We retain only the minimum data necessary to provide the service. Token data is automatically purged when a user revokes access or deletes their account.
  • No sale of data: We do not sell, rent, or share your TikTok data with any third party for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your identity via TikTok OAuth2.
  • Publish content to TikTok on your behalf, only when you explicitly initiate the action.
  • Display basic profile information within the App interface.
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • With TikTok, as required to perform API calls you have authorized.
  • When required by law or valid legal process.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted storage, HTTPS-only communication, and access controls. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Revoke the App's access to your TikTok account through your TikTok account settings.
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you by contacting us.
  • Access or export your data upon request.

7. Children's Privacy

Insights Extract is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@insightsextract.app
